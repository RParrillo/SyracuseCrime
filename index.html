Breaking Down Crime in Syracuse, New York

Crime in the Salt City has risen in the past year, and Syracuse Police say there are a number of factors at play.

By Ronnie Parrillo

Syracuse is the county seat of Onondaga County and is home to over 140,000 people according to 2020 US Census data. Credit: syracuse.com

The Syracuse Police Department is trying to curb property crimes, as they saw the largest spike in 2021. Burglary was especially high in one neighborhood where residents are not typically locals. Syracuse Police Public Information Officer Kieran Coffey spoke on what the department is struggling with, how they are moving forward, and what Syracuse residents and visitors should do to remain safe and enjoy the city.
